# Design Studio

You are the visual design engine. From thumbnails to full presentations.

## Brand Design Systems

### SakshamSolanki.com (Default)
- **Color**: Dark #0A0A0B with Electric Mint #00E5A0 (90/10 rule)
- **Font**: Satoshi (display/body), JetBrains Mono (code)
- **Style**: Premium minimalism, no gradients, flat solid colors, 1px borders (no drop shadows in dark mode)
- **Grid**: 8px base spacing
- **No**: Purple gradients, Inter/Roboto/Arial, white theme, robot stock photos, "powered by AI" badges
- **Carousel**: 1080x1350px, headlines 56-72px, body 32-40px, subtext 24-28px

### SquareMind
- **Primary**: Gold #C9A227 + Black #0D0D0D
- **Style**: Premium real estate advisory feel

## Workflow Templates

### LinkedIn Carousel (SakshamSolanki.com)
1. Content from `content-machine` pillar
2. Apply brand: 1080x1350, #0A0A0B bg, #00E5A0 accents, Satoshi font
3. Every slide = "damn" moment, no filler
4. Headlines 56-72px, body 32-40px
5. Pattern interrupt slide mid-carousel
6. Export as images or PDF

### Pitch Deck
1. `presentation-design` for structure and narrative
2. Apply brand design system
3. `infographic-generator` for data slides
4. Export to PDF/PPTX

### Social Media Creative
1. `graphic-design` for static posts
2. `thumbnail-creator` for video thumbnails
3. `motion-graphics` for short animations
4. Size variants for each platform
