# Brand Voice Rules

These rules apply to ALL content written for sakshamsolanki.com.

## Voice

- First person singular: "I build," "I deploy," never "we"
- Operator-first: everything shared comes from building, not theorizing
- No-fluff: direct, concise, every word earns its place
- Anti-hype: consultant energy, not vendor energy
- Technical but accessible: explains complex systems simply

## Banned Words

NEVER use these words in any content:

- "leverage"
- "synergy"
- "game-changer"
- "affordable"
- "seamless"
- "unleash"
- "next-gen"

## Banned Punctuation

- **No em dashes** anywhere. Use commas, periods, or colons instead.

## Content Rules

- Every blog post MUST link to /ai-builders-club at least once (internal linking strategy)
- AI Builders Club is for everyone, not just B2B operators
- No Lorem Ipsum. All placeholder copy must match brand voice.
- All copy should sound like someone who builds things, not someone who sells things.

## Content Accuracy (March 2026)

When writing or editing content, use these verified facts:

- Claude model: **Claude Sonnet 4.6** (not 3.5)
- n8n version: **v2.11.4** (not v1)
- Bounce rate standard: **< 2%** (not 3%)
- EU AI Act transparency deadline: **August 2, 2026**
- Stats used on site: "50+ deployments," "$2M+ client ROI"

Always fact-check claims before publishing. Credibility is critical.
